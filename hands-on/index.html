The Start of My Love For Art

I was born and raised in Portland, Oregon. I've always had a love for art and I was put in classes young. As I grew my love for art has grown. Now I've been doing art for my whole life really.

My Life in Photos

Art is imitating life is a quote that has always stayed with me. We use art as a tool to help us understand or cope with the things going on in our life or the emotions we face. Good art is simply emotion materialized. By showing these photos I hope to show you some pieces of me.
